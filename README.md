# Erp_hospital_management
# 🏥 ERP Solution for Private Hospital Management System

A comprehensive SAP ERP solution designed for a private multi-specialty hospital, integrating **Sales & Distribution (SD)**, **Materials Management (MM)**, and **Production Planning (PP)** to streamline hospital operations, improve patient care, and automate business processes.

---

## 📖 Overview

Traditional hospitals often struggle with disconnected departments, manual workflows, inventory issues, and billing errors. This project demonstrates how SAP ERP can unify these operations into a centralized platform.

The system integrates:

- 🩺 Patient Registration & Billing
- 💊 Medicine Procurement & Inventory
- 🧪 Laboratory & Diagnostic Planning
- 📊 Financial Integration
- 👨‍⚕️ Staff Management

---

## 🚀 Features

### Sales & Distribution (SD)
- Patient registration as Customer Master
- Sales Inquiry & Quotation
- Sales Order Creation
- Billing Management
- Service Delivery Tracking

### Materials Management (MM)
- Vendor Management
- Medicine Master
- Purchase Requisition
- RFQ & Quotation Management
- Purchase Orders
- Goods Receipt
- Inventory Tracking
- Supplier Invoice Processing

### Production Planning (PP)
- Lab Test Scheduling
- Resource Planning
- Sales & Operations Planning (SOP)
- Material Requirements Planning (MRP)
- Production Order Management
- Workflow Management
